üìñ How to Play

  • Select a seed from the shop
  • Click on an empty plot to plant
  • Water your plants to help them grow
  • Use fertilizer for extra growth boost
  • Use shovel to remove unwanted plants or sprinklers
  • Buy and place sprinklers to boost plant growth
  • Harvest when plants are fully grown
  • Earn money to buy sprinklers and upgrade tools!

üîß Tool Upgrades

  • Water Tool: Reduces cooldown by 1s per level, +20% growth bonus per level, +10 water per level
  • Fertilizer Tool: Reduces cooldown by 1.5s per level, +30% growth bonus per level, +5 fertilizer per level
  • Harvest Tool: +10% money bonus per level when harvesting
  • Shovel Tool: No special effects (cosmetic upgrade)

üíß Sprinkler System

  • Basic Sprinkler: +20% growth speed, 1 tile range
  • Advanced Sprinkler: +40% growth, +10% water efficiency, 2 tile range
  • Premium Sprinkler: +60% growth, +20% water, +10% fertilizer, 2 tile range
  • Legendary Sprinkler: +80% growth, +30% water, +20% fertilizer, 3 tile range
  • Sprinklers affect all plants within their range
  • Multiple sprinklers stack their effects
  • Use shovel tool to remove placed sprinklers

üå§Ô∏è Weather Effects

  • Sunny: Normal growth speed (1.0x)
  • Rainy: 50% faster growth (1.5x)
  • Cloudy: 20% slower growth (0.8x)
  • Stormy: 100% faster growth (2.0x)

üå± Seasonal Effects

  • Spring: 20% faster growth (1.2x) - Best for spring crops
  • Summer: Normal growth speed (1.0x) - Best for summer crops
  • Fall: 20% slower growth (0.8x) - Best for fall crops
  • Winter: 40% slower growth (0.6x) - Limited crop availability
  • Seasons change every 5 real-world days
  • Some seeds are only available in specific seasons
  • Year-round seeds can be planted in any season
